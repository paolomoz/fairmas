Maximize Hotel Profitability with Fairmas & HotStats: A Collaborative Infographic

Kinza Raheel | January 22, 2025 | 2 min

Discover the power of seamless collaboration between Fairmas and HotStats to revolutionize your hotel’s financial planning and performance analysis. This infographic highlights how these two industry leaders empower hotels to plan strategically, define performance metrics, and adopt a holistic approach to decision-making. From enhancing hotel-wide and group-wide planning to enabling actionable insights, Fairmas and HotStats deliver software solutions to drive profitability and efficiency.

Explore how Fairmas and HotStats support your hotel’s success by combining data-driven insights with robust planning solutions, helping you take charge of your financial future.

How the Fairmas and HotStats collaboration works

The Fairmas and HotStats 2-way data flow allows hotels to push financial data to HotStats and pull key performance indicators and competitor data from HotStats. One of the standout features of this integration is the ability to view HotStats benchmarking data directly within FairPlanner, Fairmas’ Financial Planning and Analysis (FP&A) software. This means you no longer need to log into HotStats separately to access crucial performance metrics.

Hotels can access the most utilized hospitality KPIs of their selected compset within FairPlanner. The user can choose which compset to use for data pulls from the HotStats API, change their selection, and refresh their data at any time. This powerful integration improves financial visibility and strategic decision-making, tailored specifically for the hospitality industry. By providing comprehensive performance insights, it increases accuracy and efficiency, helping your hotel make more informed strategic decisions.
