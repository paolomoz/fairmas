USALI 12 – Latest Update 2024

Kinza Raheel | July 11, 2024 | 2 min

*Disclaimer: Hospitality Financial and Technology Professionals (HFTP®) is the publisher and copyright owner of USALI. Fairmas only uses the term “USALI” to refer to the existing industry standard as one of several, possible, company-specific P&L account frameworks.

At Fairmas, we are excited to share the key changes introduced in the 12th Edition of USALI (Uniform System of Accounts for the Lodging Industry) by HFTP. This article highlights the latest USALI 12 updates for 2024 that every hotel finance professional should know.

At Fairmas, we continue the commitment to keeping you ahead of the curve and empowering your financial planning and analysis.

We understand the importance of staying well-informed about industry standards and trends. That’s why we ensure to keep you updated on everything well in advance, making you ready for the future of hospitality finance. Our proactive approach guarantees that you are equipped with the knowledge and tools needed to seamlessly integrate new standards, making adaptation a breeze.

The new edition of USALI , USALI 12 will include the following major changes:

Major Changes:

  • Operating Statements
  • Rooms – Schedule 1
  • Executive/Club Lounge Cost Schedule – 1-1
  • Energy, Water, & Waste – Schedule 9

Further clarifications, updates and minor changes are made within Operating Statements as well.
