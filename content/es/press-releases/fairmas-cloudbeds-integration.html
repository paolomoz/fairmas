Fairmas and Cloudbeds Announce Integration to Drive Financial and Operational Efficiency for Hoteliers

Germany / USA | 25/09/2025 | 2 minutos

Comunicado de prensa disponible solo en inglés.

Fairmas, the leading provider of financial planning, controlling, and management reporting software solutions for the global hospitality industry, and Cloudbeds, the AI-powered platform fueling hotel growth, are proud to announce the launch of their integration.

This collaboration enables seamless integration between Cloudbeds’ PMS and Fairmas, giving hoteliers an unprecedented level of accuracy, efficiency, and insight in managing their business.

Through the new interface, hotel operators can automatically transfer daily actuals from Cloudbeds’ PMS into Fairmas’ financial planning and reporting software solution. This eliminates manual data entry, improves data reliability, and allows hoteliers to focus on proactive financial decision-making.

“Hoteliers need financial clarity they can trust. By bringing together Cloudbeds’ intelligent platform with Fairmas’ proven planning tools, we’re eliminating manual work and data silos, giving operators a single source of truth. This integration empowers hoteliers to make faster, smarter decisions that strengthen profitability and unlock long-term growth.” said Sebastien Leitner, VP Partnerships, Cloudbeds

Fairmas’ software solutions have long been trusted by hotel operators and investors worldwide for financial planning, controlling, and management reporting. By integrating with Cloudbeds’ PMS, a platform redefining modern hotel operations, distribution, guest experience, and analytics, hoteliers of all sizes now gain access to a unified ecosystem that bridges operational performance with financial strategy.

“At Fairmas, our mission has always been to equip hoteliers with reliable, forward-looking financial tools that drive success,” said Oliver Rabe, Chief Commercial Officer, Fairmas “Our integration with Cloudbeds ensures that hotel operators can seamlessly connect their operational data with Fairmas’ financial planning solutions, enabling smarter decisions and sustainable growth.”

With this partnership, both companies continue their shared commitment to innovation in hospitality technology. By aligning operational and financial intelligence, Fairmas and Cloudbeds enable hotels to not only streamline their processes but also unlock new opportunities for profitability, resilience, and long-term success in an increasingly competitive industry.

About Fairmas

Fairmas is a leading provider of financial planning, controlling, and management reporting software solutions worldwide, designed for the hotel industry. Since 2003, Fairmas has supported hoteliers and investors worldwide, with 18,000+ users across 5,500+ hotels. Its web-based BI solutions integrate seamlessly with 150+ leading PMS, accounting, and revenue management systems, helping hotels achieve financial goals, optimize profitability, and ensure sustainable growth.
