Rolf Arndt Named Managing Director of Fairmas Services GmbH

Kinza Raheel | 08/04/2024 | 2 minutos

Este comunicado de prensa sólo está disponible en inglés:

Berlin, Germany – 4/8/2024

Fairmas proudly announces the appointment of Rolf Arndt as the Managing Director of its subsidiary, Fairmas Services GmbH, the Fairmas branch specialised in providing additional services for hotel owners & investors. Notably, Fairmas remains under the leadership of Niels Schroeder, while Rolf Arndt will lead the Fairmas Services GmbH. With over 30 years of invaluable experience in the hospitality industry, particularly in Finance, Rolf brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to his new role.

In his capacity as Managing Director, Rolf will lead Fairmas Services, guiding an innovative solution that reshapes asset management in the hospitality sector. “Modern Data Management for Hospitality Assets,” is the main offering provided by Fairmas Services, designed to connect hotel investors and operators. Fairmas Services provides web-based services for standardized & consolidated Portfolio Performance Reporting. They deal with the collection and validation processes of data management allowing hotel investors, owners and asset managers to focus on the evaluation and analysis of the appropriately prepared data.

Prior to his role at Fairmas Services, Rolf served at IHG Hotels & Resorts as a Director for global hotel-owner planning and reporting processes. His extensive background includes strategic business planning, advanced analytical skills, and proficient project management for both regional and global finance initiatives.

“I am honoured to lead Fairmas Services into this new era of innovation and growth,” said Rolf. “My focus will be on driving excellence through our services, particularly building a bridge between hotel investors, owners, asset managers and operators, so our customers can focus on what really matters.”

Rolf Arndt, Managing Director, Fairmas Services

Rolf has a proven track record of success and leadership in diverse environments, such as implementing several hotel finance reporting and planning systems for 950+ hotels globally, labour management system including productivity benchmarking in various markets. He has also led the global hotel finance support for more than 3000 system users and is an experienced global team leader. His vast experience in the field makes him the ideal candidate to spearhead Fairmas Services.

Niels Schroeder, Fairmas’ Managing Director, is eagerly anticipating the future growth and success of Fairmas Services in partnership with Fairmas.

“Rolf’s expertise and leadership will be invaluable as we continue to innovate and expand our services at Fairmas. We are thrilled to welcome him to Fairmas Services and look forward to achieving new milestones together, by providing more customized services for the special needs of hotel investors and owners.”

Niels Schroeder, Managing Director, Fairmas GmbH

Rolf and the Fairmas Services team strive in their mission to drive excellence and innovation in the hospitality industry.
