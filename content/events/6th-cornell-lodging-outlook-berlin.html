Fairmas will attend the 6th Cornell Lodging Outlook Berlin, hosted by the Cornell Hotel Society German Chapter. This industry forum brings together hoteliers, investors, and leaders to explore market outlooks, strategy, and performance trends shaping European hospitality.

When: January 23, 2026
Where: Hotel Adlon Kempinski Berlin, Germany

As a company specializing in hotel-specific financial planning and reporting software, Fairmas helps hospitality professionals strengthen financial control, improve forecasting accuracy, and make data-driven decisions that support long-term performance and resilience.

At the Cornell Lodging Outlook Berlin, Fairmas looks forward to:

  • Participating in forward-looking discussions about the evolving hospitality landscape
  • Demonstrating how accurate budgeting, forecasting, and reporting can support strategic decision-making
  • Networking with industry peers during the reception and dinner to exchange insights on performance challenges and opportunities

Meet Fairmas’ Wolfgang Gattringer to explore how robust financial planning tools can help your hotel navigate change and drive sustainable growth.
