AAHOACON – AAHOA Annual Convention & Trade Show

April 8 @ 8:00 am - April 10 @ 5:00 pm UTC+2

Fairmas is excited to be an exhibitor at AAHOACON26 – the AAHOA Annual Convention & Trade Show, the largest gathering of hotel owners and industry leaders in the United States. This three-day event features high-impact education sessions, an expansive trade show,
networking opportunities, and strategic insights aimed at enhancing profitability, leadership, and long-term value for hotel owners.

As a company focused on hotel financial planning and reporting software, Fairmas supports owners and operators with budgeting, forecasting, and financial transparency that align with operational goals and investment strategy.

When: April 8–10, 2026
Where: Pennsylvania Convention Center, Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, USA

Connect with Fairmas at Booth 336 to explore how structured financial planning can support your hotel operations and profitability objectives. Book your meeting with Eric Healy and Tahil Mascarenhas from our Fairmas North America team today.
