The 2nd ACE Hotel Tech Summit in Dubai promises an immersive exploration of the latest products, technological advancements, and sustainability initiatives within the hospitality sector. Featuring esteemed speakers from across the industry, the summit’s agenda boasts compelling keynotes, global CEO panels, and personalized sessions. Attendees can anticipate engaging with industry leaders, top investors, and subject matter experts, fostering meaningful conversations and valuable networking opportunities. Catering to hoteliers poised to distinguish their establishments, the summit offers access to over 120 companies, ensuring attendees find the ideal partners to propel their businesses forward.

Meet Dima Ladizhenski and learn from him how Fairmas’ hotel financial planning, controlling and reporting software can help in increasing your hotel’s profit.
