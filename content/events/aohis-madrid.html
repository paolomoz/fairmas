AOHIS (Atlantic Ocean Hotel Investors Summit) is a premier event for hotel real estate investors, developers, and franchisees, focusing on networking and investment trends in the Atlantic regions, including Iberia, the Americas, and Africa. It emphasizes high-level, owner-driven discussions. It will take place on January 19-20 at the NH Collection Eurobuilding Hotel in Madrid, Spain.

Fairmas representative for Spain Carla Richart and Fairmas Services’ Rolf Arndt will be on the ground once again to meet with hotel owners and investors looking for financial planning, reporting and data management for hotel asset solutions that support informed investment and operational decisions in the industry.
