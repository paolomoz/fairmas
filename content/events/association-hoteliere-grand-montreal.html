Fairmas is pleased to attend the AHGM Hotel Forecasts 2026–2027 event in Montréal, represented by Eric Healy and John Dunn. Organized by the Association hôtelière du Grand Montréal (AHGM), this annual conference brings together hotel owners, operators, investors, and industry experts to explore the economic outlook, hotel performance trends, and the key factors shaping the hospitality market in the year ahead.

Dates: July 14, 2026
Location: Hôtel Place d’Armes, Montréal

Why Meet Fairmas:
Connect with Eric at the Fairmas stand to discuss how Fairmas helps hotels and hotel groups transform financial data into strategic insights through integrated budgeting, forecasting, financial planning, and management reporting. Learn how our hospitality-focused FP&A solutions support confident decision-making in an evolving market.

Whether you’re a hotel owner, operator, or finance leader, stop by the Fairmas booth and find out how we can help you stay ahead in a dynamic market.

Book your meeting today with the Fairmas North America Team – Eric Healy.
