Fairmas will be exhibiting at the British Columbia Hotel Association (BCHA) Summit & Marketplace 2026, a leading regional hospitality event uniting hoteliers, operators, and industry partners across British Columbia. The Summit features educational sessions, a vibrant Marketplace vendor exhibition, and industry awards celebrating excellence in the sector.

Fairmas provides hotel-specific financial planning and reporting tools that help hoteliers align financial strategy with operational performance, improve budget accuracy, and support growth.

When: April 14–15, 2026
Where: Vancouver Island Conference Centre, Nanaimo, British Columbia, Canada

Book your meeting with Eric Healy of our Fairmas North America team at the Fairmas booth, to explore how structured financial planning supports performance, profitability, and strategic decision-making.
