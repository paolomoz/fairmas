style

Fairmas is pleased to take part in the Connect: Hospitality Marketplace by AHP, organized by the Portuguese Hotel Association. This one-day forum brings together top hotel professionals and trusted suppliers in a structured, goal‑oriented environment designed to foster real business connections.

As a specialist software provider of hotel‑specific financial planning and reporting solutions, Fairmas is committed to helping hoteliers improve profitability, enhance forecasting accuracy, and elevate operational efficiency.

When: October 8, 2025
Where: Meliá Lisboa Aeroporto, Lisbon, Portugal

Why meet Fairmas?

  • Explore hands-on demonstrations: See live renditions of our budgeting, forecasting, and reporting tools tailored specifically for hotels.
  • Strengthen your financial performance: Discover how Fairmas enables hotel teams to make smarter investment and operational decisions, adjust to market shifts, and deliver enhanced results.
  • Connect directly with us: Take advantage of 1:1 meeting opportunities to discuss your challenges and explore bespoke financial management solutions.

Book your meeting with Carla Richart, Fairmas Business Development Manager for Iberia & LATAM, at Table 15. She looks forward to connecting face-to-face with hoteliers and industry leaders who would like to transform their hotel’s financial planning.
