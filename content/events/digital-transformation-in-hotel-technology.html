Fairmas is excited to attend Digital Transformation in Hotel Technology 2026, Europe’s premier hotel tech conference bringing together leading hoteliers, technology innovators, and industry strategists to redefine how hotels operate in the digital age. This two-day event offers a unique blend of high-value insights, case studies, and networking opportunities focused on the technologies that are shaping the future of hospitality.

When: February 4–5, 2026
Where: Hotel Le Negresco, Nice, France

As a provider of hotel-specific financial planning and reporting software, Fairmas helps hoteliers enhance forecasting accuracy, improve budgeting processes, and tie financial strategy directly to operational performance — a key advantage as hotels implement digital transformation initiatives.

At the conference, Fairmas looks forward to:

  • Exploring how hotel finance teams can leverage tech insights to strengthen planning and performance in a rapidly evolving landscape
  • Connecting with hotel leaders and tech innovators to discuss how financial planning can support digital adoption and operational agility
  • Sharing perspectives on aligning financial strategy with next-generation hotel technology
