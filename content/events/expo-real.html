Fairmas will attend EXPO REAL, Europe’s largest international trade fair for real estate and investment. Bringing together professionals from the property, finance and hospitality sectors, the event offers valuable opportunities to discuss investment strategies, market trends and the future of hotel real estate.

Date: 6 – 8 October 2026
Venue: Messe München

Why Meet Fairmas:

Whether you’re evaluating new investments or optimising existing assets, we’d be pleased to discuss how Fairmas helps hospitality businesses make better financial decisions through reliable data and integrated planning and reporting. Connect with Fairmas to discover how our hospitality-focused FP&A solutions help hotel owners, investors and asset managers improve budgeting, forecasting, financial planning and management reporting across their portfolios.

Book your meeting with Oliver Rabe who will be on the ground to meet with potential and existing customers, and will present Hotel Asset Reporting, Fairmas’ product-service solution to potential new customers who need support in helping them achieve their financial goals.
