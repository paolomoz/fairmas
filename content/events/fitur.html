January 21 - January 23

FITUR (Feria Internacional de Turismo) is a leading global event for tourism professionals and the principal trade fair for both inbound and outbound markets in Latin America. It serves as a vital meeting point for tourism professionals worldwide and is considered the premier business platform for the tourism industry.

The upcoming FITUR 2026 is scheduled to take place from January 21 to 25, 2026, at IFEMA Madrid. The first three days (January 21-23) dedicated exclusively to professionals and the weekend days open to the public.

Fairmas, the software provider specializing in hotel financial planning, controlling, and management reporting, will be represented on the ground by Carla Richart, Business Development Manager for Spain, Portugal and Latin America. She will be meeting potential new customers to showcase Fairmas’ solutions, update existing customers and partners of latest developments and stay abreast of the latest trends and developments in the tourism and hospitality sector, from January 21-23 (for professionals).

Are you attending FITUR as well? Book your on-site meeting with Carla.
