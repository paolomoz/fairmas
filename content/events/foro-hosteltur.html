Fairmas attends Foro Hosteltur, a leading hospitality and tourism forum in Spain that brings together top industry executives to discuss market trends, digital transformation, sustainability, and the future of travel.

Following several years of robust growth, a resurgence in demand, and enhancements in key profitability metrics, the tourism sector is now confronted with a more intricate landscape. Geopolitical tensions, macroeconomic fluctuations, rising operational costs, and uncertainties in specific source markets are reshaping the competitive landscape. In this new environment, foresight and adaptability emerge as critical advantages. Under the slogan “Analysing the present, projecting the future”, this year’s Foro Hosteltur, part of the discussion is aimed at addressing with an executive approach the keys that will mark the roadmap of tourism companies in the coming months.

Date: May 8, 2026
Venue: Hotel Meliá Castilla

Meet Carla Richart, Business Development Manager for Spain and LatAm, who will be on the ground at Foro Hosteltur . Learn how Fairmas supports hotels withefficient financial planning, budgeting, and reporting to drive better business decisions.
