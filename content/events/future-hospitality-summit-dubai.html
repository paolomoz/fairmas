Fairmas is exhibiting at Future Hospitality Summit (FHS) Dubai, one of the Middle East’s leading hospitality investment conferences. Bringing together hotel investors, owners, operators, developers and technology providers, FHS explores the trends, innovations and investment opportunities driving the future of hospitality across the region.

When: September 29 – October 1, 2026
Where: Madinat Jumeirah, Dubai, United Arab Emirates

Why meet Fairmas?

Visit Fairmas at the ExploreTech’s Tech Pavillion to explore how our hotel-specific FP&A solutions help hospitality businesses improve budgeting, forecasting, financial planning and management reporting while supporting sustainable growth and operational excellence.

Book your meeting with either Dima Ladizhenski, Fairmas Regional Manager for Middle East, Africa and Asia, as well we as Rolf Arndt, Managing Director of Fairmas Services, specialized in Hotel Asset Reporting, as they show how Fairmas can help you thrive in the evolving world of hospitality financial operations and investment.
