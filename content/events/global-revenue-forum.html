Fairmas is excited to exhibit at the Global Revenue Forum – Milan 2026, the premier gathering for commercial and revenue management leaders in hospitality. Held in one of Europe’s most dynamic hotel markets, this full-day event brings together revenue managers, general managers, hotel owners, and commercial experts to explore the future of commercial leadership in hospitality.

When: January 29, 2026
Where: Meliá Milano, Milan, Italy

As a provider of hotel-specific financial planning and reporting software, Fairmas is committed to helping hotels translate revenue strategy into strong financial performance. At GRF Milan, we look forward to:

  • Engaging with commercial leaders and experts on emerging revenue strategies and innovation
  • Demonstrating how Fairmas can support smarter, data-driven financial planning aligned with progressive revenue models
  • Connecting with industry peers to discuss challenges, share insights, and explore new opportunities for growth

Meet Chiara Gianformaggio and Cornelia Kolm at our Fairmas stand and they would be happy to show you how we in Fairmas can help you bridge revenue and finance for sustainable success.
