Fairmas is excited to announce our participation in the Greater Toronto Hotel Association (GTHA) Business Outlook Forum 2025, a highly anticipated half-day event bringing together key leaders from Toronto’s hotel community to discuss market trends, future outlooks, and strategies for growth.

As a leading software company providing hotel-specific financial planning and reporting solutions, Fairmas supports hoteliers in optimizing profitability, improving forecasting accuracy, and making data-driven strategic decisions.

When: September 15, 2025
Where: Hilton Toronto Hotel

Why meet Fairmas?

  • See our solutions live: Visit our booth to experience hands-on demonstrations of our tailored budgeting, forecasting, and reporting software designed exclusively for hotels.
  • Drive financial success: Learn how Fairmas can help your property strengthen its financial strategy, improve planning, and enhance overall performance.
  • Connect with our team: Take this opportunity to meet our experts in Toronto, discuss your business challenges, and explore customized solutions to support your goals in 2025 and beyond.

Meet Eric Healy at the Fairmas booth and discover how Fairmas can help future-proof your hotel’s financial success.
