Fairmas is pleased to attend the Gulf & Indian Ocean Hotel Investors’ Summit (GIOHS) Dubai 2026, a focused forum for hotel investors, owners, developers, and C-suite leaders to connect and explore investment opportunities across the Gulf and Indian Ocean region.

When: October 26 – 27, 2026
Where: Anantara Downtown Dubai Hotel, UAE

Meet Fairmas to discuss financial planning strategies that support investment decisions and long-term hotel performance.

Book a meeting with Dima Ladizhenski and he will help you discover how Fairmas can support your business growth.
