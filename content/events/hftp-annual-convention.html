Fairmas is proud to announce our participation in the HFTP Annual Convention 2025, a premier event uniting hospitality finance and technology professionals from around the globe. It is also an occasion to celebrate the association and recognize the accomplishments of members for that year. As a Corporate Allied Sponsor, Fairmas proudly supports HFTP’s mission to advance innovation and education in the hospitality sector.

Fairmas is a leading software company specializing in hotel-specific financial planning and reporting solutions, empowering hotels to optimize their financial performance, improve planning accuracy, and make smarter strategic decisions.

When: October 22–24, 2025
Where: Rosen Centre Hotel, Orlando, Florida, USA

Why meet Fairmas?

  • See our solutions in action: Discover live demonstrations of our dedicated budgeting, forecasting, and reporting software designed exclusively for hotels.
  • Strengthen your financial strategy: Learn how our solutions help hotel finance teams increase profitability and gain better control over operational and investment decisions.
  • Connect with our team: As a committed Corporate Allied Sponsor, we’re excited to engage with hospitality professionals in Orlando, share insights, and explore customized solutions to meet your unique business goals.

Book your meeting with Eric Healy at our table top exhibit and see how Fairmas can help you build a stronger, more resilient financial future for your hotel or portfolio.
