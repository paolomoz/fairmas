Fairmas is excited to exhibit at HOSPACE 2026, HOSPA’s annual conference bringing together leaders in hospitality finance, technology and commercial strategy. The event offers valuable insights into industry developments while providing an excellent platform for networking and knowledge sharing across the UK hospitality sector.

Date: November 12, 2026
Venue: Royal Lancaster, London

Why meet Fairmas?

Visit the Fairmas Booth 25 or arrange a meeting during HOSPACE to discover how Fairmas helps hotels simplify budgeting, forecasting, financial planning and management reporting with solutions built exclusively for the hospitality industry. Whether you’re looking to streamline financial processes or improve strategic planning across your hotel portfolio, we’d be delighted to show you how Fairmas can help.

Schedule your meeting today withAlexander Engels and Sophie Wuttke, and learn how Fairmas can transform your approach to hotel financial planning.
