Fairmas is pleased to exhibit at Hospitality Day 2026, Italy’s largest educational event dedicated to hotel professionals. Bringing together hotel owners, general managers, revenue managers, finance professionals and technology providers, Hospitality Day offers a full day of seminars, workshops and networking focused on the future of hospitality management and innovation.

Date: October 13, 2025
Venue: Palacongressi di Rimini, Rimini, Italy

Why meet Fairmas?

Visit the Fairmas booth to discover how our hospitality-specific Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A) solutions help hotels improve budgeting, forecasting, financial planning and management reporting. Learn how integrated financial data supports better operational and strategic decision-making.

Whether you’re looking to modernise your financial planning processes or gain greater visibility into hotel performance, we’d be delighted to meet you and discuss how Fairmas can support your business. Stop by our booth or arrange a meeting with our team.

Book your meeting with Cornelia Kolm and Chiara Gianformaggio today and see how Fairmas can help elevate your hotel’s financial strategy from the inside out.
