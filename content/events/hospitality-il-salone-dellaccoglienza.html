February 3 @ 10:00 am - February 4 @ 5:00 pm UTC+2

style
event-head

Fairmas is pleased to attend Hospitality – Il Salone dell’Accoglienza, one of Europe’s most influential trade fairs dedicated entirely to the hospitality industry. The event brings together hoteliers, operators, consultants, and technology providers from across Europe to explore innovation, operational excellence, and the future of hotel management.

As a provider of hotel-specific financial planning and reporting software, Fairmas supports hotels in building reliable budgets, improving forecast accuracy, and gaining deeper financial transparency across departments and properties. In a market shaped by rising costs and evolving guest expectations, structured financial planning has never been more critical.

When: February 2 – 5, 2026
Where: Exhibition Center Riva del Garda, Italy

At Hospitality – Il Salone dell’Accoglienza, Fairmas looks forward to:

  • Exchanging insights with hospitality professionals on financial planning challenges and best practices
  • Demonstrating how data-driven budgeting and forecasting can strengthen operational and strategic decision-making
  • Connecting with hotel groups and independent operators seeking greater control over performance and profitability

Join us to explore how modern financial planning can support sustainable hotel success.

Book your meeting with Chiara Gianformaggio as she will be onsite From February 3 – 4.
