Fairmas is attending HospitalitySud 2026, the premier hospitality trade fair in Southern Italy dedicated to hotel and extra-hotel professionals. HospitalitySud brings together owners, managers, staff, and consultants from hotels, resorts, B&Bs, agritourism, glamping and more to explore products, services, and trends shaping modern hospitality.

When: February 19-20, 2026
Where: Maritime Station – Naples

At the two-day event, the Fairmas team will be available to:

  • Connect with hospitality operators, partners and industry stakeholders
  • Share insights on hotel financial planning, budgeting, and performance management
  • Discuss current trends and opportunities in hospitality and tourism

Book your meeting with Cornelia Kolm and Chiara Gianformaggio to exchange ideas on how data-driven financial planning can empower hotels and hospitality businesses.
