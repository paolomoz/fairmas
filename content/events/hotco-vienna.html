HOTCO Vienna is an international conference focused on hotel development and investment opportunities, particularly in Central and Eastern Europe (CEE). Scheduled for January 20-21, 2025, at the Andaz Vienna am Belvedere Hotel in Austria, the event brings together key stakeholders in the hospitality industry, including investors, developers, hotel companies, and financial institutions, to discuss trends and strategies shaping the future of the sector. Fairmas, a software provider specializing in hotel financial planning, controlling, and management reporting, is likely to send a delegate to HOTCO to connect with decision-makers, showcase its financial tools, and explore potential partnerships. By participating, Fairmas aims to position itself as a leader in financial software solutions, gain valuable industry insights, and expand its market reach in the region.
