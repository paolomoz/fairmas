Fairmas, represented by Dima Ladizhenski, is pleased to take part in the Hotel Revolution Summit, a leading global forum dedicated to new strategies, technologies, and business models reshaping the hospitality landscape. Bringing together hoteliers, investors, operators, and solution providers, the Summit serves as a hub for forward-looking discussions on the future of hotel performance, operations, and investment.

When: January 20, 2026
Where: Dubai, UAE

Fairmas specializes in hotel-specific financial planning and reporting software, helping hoteliers make data-driven decisions and maximize profitability. At the Summit, we look forward to:

  • Learning how technology and strategy are evolving in hospitality
  • Exploring opportunities to align our financial tools with emerging hotel trends
  • Networking with forward-thinking hoteliers and investors

Meet us there — we’d love to discuss how Fairmas can support your hotel’s financial future.
