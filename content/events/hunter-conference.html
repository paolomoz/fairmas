Fairmas is pleased to exhibit at the Hunter Conference 2026, the hospitality industry’s premier investment and networking event, bringing together hotel owners, developers, investors and industry leaders. Join us in Atlanta to explore how Fairmas’s advanced hotel financial planning, budgeting, forecasting and reporting solutions empower hoteliers and investors with data-driven insights to optimise profitability and strategic decision-making. Our team will be on-site to provide live demonstrations and one-to-one consultations tailored to your business needs.

Whether you’re looking to streamline financial processes or strengthen financial performance across your portfolio, meet the Fairmas experts at the Hunter Conference to discover the latest in hospitality finance technology and best practices.

When: March 16-18, 2026
Where: Signia by Hilton Atlanta
Fairmas Booth: S-5

During the event, the Fairmas team is on the ground to:

  • Network with travel and hospitality leaders
  • Share insights on hotel financial planning, budgeting, and performance management
  • Discuss industry trends shaping the future of tourism and hospitality
  • Explore partnership opportunities with hotels, groups, and service providers

Book a meeting today with Eric Healy and Tahil Mascarenhas of our Fairmas North America team and take your hotel financial strategy to the next level.
