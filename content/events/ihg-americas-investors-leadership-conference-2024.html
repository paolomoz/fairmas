IHG Americas Investors & Leadership Conference 2024

November 19, 2024 @ 8:00 am - November 20, 2024 @ 2:00 pm UTC-8

style
event-head

The IHG Americas Investors & Leadership Conference 2024 will take place from November 18-20, 2024, at The Venetian® Resort in Las Vegas, Nevada. This key industry event is designed for IHG owners, general managers, and directors of sales, featuring sessions, networking opportunities, and a tradeshow to explore innovations and best practices in hospitality management, as well as technological advancements.

Fairmas, a long-time software provider of IHG since 2005 and supporting over 1,000 IHG hotel properties worldwide to-date, will be one of the sponsors / exhibitors at the conference. Fairmas offers comprehensive financial planning, controlling, and reporting software specifically tailored for hotels. By participating, Fairmas aims to engage with even more IHG properties, showcasing how their solutions can improve financial transparency, operational efficiency, and decision-making. The company hopes that attendees will be inspired to implement their product across additional IHG properties.

Fairmas’ presence at the tradeshow (November 19-20) aligns perfectly with their mission to expand their reach within the IHG network, leveraging the event as a platform for demonstrations and direct interactions with potential users from across the Americas.

Meet Eric Healy and Eduardo Burkard of the Fairmas North America team at Booth 366. Book your meeting here.
