Fairmas is a Gold Sponsor and will be exhibiting at the International Hospitality Investment Forum (IHIF) Berlin 2026, one of the most influential global gatherings of hospitality investors, developers, operators, and strategic leaders. Over three days of panels, networking, and deal-making, IHIF explores opportunities, investment trends, and business models shaping the industry’s future.

With specialised financial planning and reporting software for hotels, especially with theFairmas Hotel Asset Reporting, Fairmas supports investment-grade budgeting, portfolio forecasting, and performance reporting — helping leadership teams make informed investment and operational decisions.

When: March 23–25, 2026
Where: InterContinental Berlin & Pullman Berlin Schweizerhof, Berlin, Germany

Meet the team at the Fairmas stand (Stand G6 at the Potsdam Foyer, InterContinental Berlin) to discuss how enhanced financial planning tools can support investment decisions and long-term value creation.
