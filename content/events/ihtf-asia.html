International Hotel Technology Forum Asia 2024 (AHTF)

November 12, 2024 @ 7:30 am - November 13, 2024 @ 5:30 pm UTC+8

style
event-head

The International Hotel Technology Forum Asia Pacific 2024 will take place on November 12-13, 2024, at the Pan Pacific Singapore. It is a premier event focusing on innovations and technological advancements in the hospitality industry, bringing together hoteliers and technology solution providers from across the Asia-Pacific region. The forum will feature discussions on emerging trends such as AI, sustainability, ESG, and advancements in guest experiences through technology. Attendees can participate in one-on-one business meetings, case study presentations, and networking sessions

Fairmas, a provider of financial planning and reporting solutions for the hotel industry, would attend this forum to showcase its specialized tools for hotel budgeting, forecasting, and financial management. Fairmas looks forward to engage with potential hotel clients looking to streamline their financial operations with advanced software solutions, while also exploring new partnerships. The forum also offers Fairmas the opportunity to stay informed about the latest trends in hotel technology and how these innovations could be integrated into our system to benefit mutual customers.

Book your meeting with Dima Ladizhensky so he can show you how Fairmas’ hotel-specifical financial planning, controlling & reporting solutions help increase your profits.
