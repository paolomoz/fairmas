Fairmas is pleased to participate once again in the International Hotel Technology Forum (IHTF) Europe 2026, a leading gathering of hospitality technology leaders, hoteliers, and solution providers focused on advancing digital transformation and operational performance in the hotel industry.

Bringing together senior decision-makers from across Europe and beyond, the forum offers a curated programme of case studies, panel discussions, and one-to-one meetings designed to help hotels evaluate emerging technologies and strengthen their digital strategies. Topics range from data integration and automation to guest experience innovation and revenue optimization, providing actionable insights for modern hospitality businesses.

As a specialist in hotel-specific financial planning and reporting software, Fairmas supports hotels and hospitality groups in turning operational and revenue data into clear financial insights. By enabling accurate budgeting, forecasting, and performance reporting, Fairmas helps decision-makers align technology investments with financial strategy and long-term profitability.

Date: April 21–23, 2026
Venue: Mövenpick Hotel Amsterdam City Centre

Book your meeting with Fairmas’ Cornelia Kolm who will be on the ground in Amsterdam to exchange ideas on financial planning, technology investment, and performance management in an increasingly data-driven hospitality environment.
