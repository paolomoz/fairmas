The International Hotel Technology Forum Middle East & North Africa (IHTF MENA) is a premier event dedicated to showcasing the latest technological solutions aimed at enhancing guest experiences, increasing revenue, and improving operational efficiency within the hospitality industry. Scheduled for February 11–12, 2025, at the JW Marriott Marina in Dubai, this forum serves as a platform for hoteliers and technology providers to engage in insightful discussions, share successful case studies, and explore innovative networking opportunities.

Fairmas, a company specializing in hotel financial planning, controlling and management reporting software, is attending IHTF MENA to present its solutions tailored for the hospitality sector. Fairmas’ participation in the event underscores our commitment to providing hoteliers with advanced tools that enhance financial performance and operational efficiency.

Book a meeting with Dima Ladizhenski here. He looks forward to connecting with industry professionals, showcase our latest innovations, and demonstrate how our Fairmas solutions can contribute to the evolving needs of the hospitality industry in the Middle East and North Africa region.
