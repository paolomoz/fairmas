Fairmas is proud to be one of the sponsors at the upcoming International Hotel Technology Forum (IHTF) UK edition that brings together senior hotel leaders and technology innovators to explore the technologies transforming the sector.

The forum is designed for senior hospitality leaders responsible for digital transformation, operations and revenue and over one day will provide strategic insights into the future of hotel technology, real-world case studies from hotel leaders, expert discussion on AI, data, automation and guest experience and more.

As a specialist in hotel-specific financial planning and reporting software, Fairmas supports hotels and hospitality groups in turning operational and revenue data into clear financial insights. By enabling accurate budgeting, forecasting, and performance reporting, Fairmas helps decision-makers align technology investments with financial strategy and long-term profitability.

Date: June 9, 2026
Venue: CodeNode, London

Book your meeting with Fairmas Director of Sales Alexander Engels who will be on the ground for 1-1 meetings to exchange ideas on financial planning, technology investment, and performance management in an increasingly data-driven hospitality environment.
