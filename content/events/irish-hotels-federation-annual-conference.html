Irish Hotels Federation 2026 Annual Conference (IHF)

February 23 @ 10:00 am - February 24 @ 5:00 pm UTC+0

style
event-head

Fairmas is pleased to join the Irish Hotels Federation (IHF) Annual Conference & Showtel Trade Exhibition as a delegate in Killarney. This key hospitality industry event brings together hotel owners, managers, and partners from across Ireland to explore trends, challenges, and opportunities shaping the future of the hotel sector.

Date: 243 -24 February 2026
Venue: The Gleneagle Hotel, Killkarney, Ireland

As part of the event, the Fairmas team will connect with hospitality leaders to exchange insights on hotel financial planning, budgeting, forecasting, and performance management, while building meaningful relationships within the Irish hospitality community.

Alexander Engels, Director of Sales at Fairmas, looks forward to:

  • Engage and build meaningful connections with Irish hotel leaders and hospitality professionals to strengthen collaboration across the Irish market
  • Share perspectives on financial planning, forecasting, budgeting and performance management for hotels
  • Explore industry challenges and opportunities with sector stakeholders
