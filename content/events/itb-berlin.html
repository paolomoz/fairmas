ITB Berlin is one of the world’s leading travel trade shows, attracting tourism professionals, hospitality experts, and travel enthusiasts from around the globe. Held annually in Berlin, it serves as a platform for networking, showcasing innovations, and discussing the latest trends in the travel, tourism, and hospitality industries. Attendees include hotels, travel agencies, tour operators, tech companies, and other stakeholders in the global tourism ecosystem.

Fairmas, a leading provider of financial planning and reporting software for the hospitality industry, has been attending ITB Berlin annually since 2017 to showcase its innovative solutions and strengthen its presence in the global hotel market. Taking place from 3 – 5 March 2026 at the Messe Berlin Exhibition Grounds in Berlin, Germany, ITB Berlin is one of the world’s premier travel trade shows, offering Fairmas a unique opportunity to connect with industry leaders, network with hotel professionals,, connect with existing customers and partners, and demonstrate how its tools simplify financial planning, revenue forecasting, and performance reporting for hotels. By participating in ITB Berlin, Fairmas not only stays ahead of industry trends but also reinforces its reputation as a trusted partner for hoteliers seeking reliable and user-friendly financial management solutions.

Visit Fairmas at ITB Berlin 2025 at Hall 8.1 Stand 133 to learn how their software can streamline your hotel’s financial operations and drive profitability.
