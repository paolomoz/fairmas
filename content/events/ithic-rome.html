September 30 @ 8:00 am - October 1 @ 5:00 pm UTC+1

Fairmas will attend the International Tourism & Hospitality Investment Conference (ITHIC) as a delegate. Recognized as one of Europe’s leading hotel investment events, ITHIC connects investors, hotel owners, operators, asset managers and advisors to discuss opportunities, market developments and investment strategies across the hospitality sector.

When: September 30 – October 1, 2026
Where: Quark Hotel Milano

Why Meet Fairmas?

Meet with our team to discuss how Fairmas supports hotel investors and operators with integrated financial planning, budgeting, forecasting and management reporting that enables informed strategic decisions throughout the investment lifecycle.

If you’re attending ITHIC, we’d be delighted to connect and discuss how Fairmas can help your organization improve financial transparency, planning efficiency and long-term portfolio performance.

Meet Cornelia Kolm and Chiara Gianformaggio to learn more.
