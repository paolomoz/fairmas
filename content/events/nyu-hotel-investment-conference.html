The 46th Annual NYU International Hospitality Industry Investment Conference (NYU IHIIC) is the leading hospitality investment conference in the Americas attended by the world’s largest and leading hotel brands, investors, owners and operators. It is also where the accomplished leaders, visionaries and influencers of global hospitality come together to be the catalyst for positive change, and to promote the continued sustainable growth of the industry.

Attending NYU IHIIC? Book your meeting with Eduardo of our Fairmas North America team and learn about Data Management for Hotel Assets catering to the needs of hotel investors and operators, as well as Fairmas’ hotel financial planning & analysis (FP&A) and reporting solutions.
