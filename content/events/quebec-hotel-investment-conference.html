Fairmas is proud to exhibit once again at the Quebec Hotel Investment Conference (QHIC). Tailored for Quebec’s hospitality sector, this one-day event brings together hotel owners, developers, investors, and lenders for focused, practical discussions. This year’s agenda explores evolving capital conditions, rate integrity, branding strategies, and how hospitality deals are being structured in the current market.

Date: September 24, 2026
Venue: Fairmont The Queen Elizabeth, Montréal

Why Meet Fairmas?

Visit our booth to learn how Fairmas helps hotels transform financial data into meaningful business insights through integrated budgeting, forecasting, financial planning and management reporting designed specifically for the hospitality industry.

Whether you’re expanding your portfolio or looking to optimise financial performance, the Fairmas team will showcase their advanced hospitality financial BI software solutions, with a focus on the Hotel Asset Reporting solution, designed to help hotel businesses achieve financial success and boost profitability.
