Resort & Residential Hospitality Forum (R&R)

November 11, 2025 @ 1:30 pm - November 13, 2025 @ 2:30 pm UTC+2

Fairmas will be returning to the Resort & Residential (R&R) Forum by Questex, the premier event uniting hospitality, resort, and branded residential leaders and investors from across EMEA. The forum offers a focused platform to explore market opportunities, exchange strategic insights, and shape the future of resort and residential hospitality.

When: November 11–13, 2025
Where: InterContinental Athenaeum, Athens

Why meet Fairmas?

  • Strengthen your financial strategy: Discover how our specialized budgeting, forecasting, and reporting solutions, especially Hotel Asset Reporting, can help you make smarter, data-driven decisions for your hotel and resort portfolios.
  • Maximize investment value: Learn how Fairmas supports owners and operators in boosting profitability and optimizing asset performance.
  • Connect face-to-face: Take the opportunity to meet our team in Athens, discuss your business objectives, and explore tailored solutions designed for the resort and residential sector.

Book your meeting with Carla Richart and see how Fairmas can help drive the financial success of your hotel business.
