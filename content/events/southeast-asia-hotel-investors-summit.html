South East Asia Hotel Investors’ Summit (SEAHIS) 2026, a premier event for hotel real estate investors, developers, and franchisees. Join us in Bangkok to explore how our financial planning and hotel asset reporting solutions can enhance your hotel’s profitability.

Date: June 15-16, 2026

Why Meet Fairmas?

Exchange insights on financial planning, investment scenarios, and performance management for hotels in fast-growing markets.

Meet the Fairmas team – Dima Ladizhenski, Business Development Manager for Fairmas, and Rolf Arndt, Managing Director of Fairmas Services, and learn about Fairmas’ Hotel Asset Reporting and the other financial planning, controlling and reporting solutions that can support your business growth! Book your meeting today.

What Makes SEAHIS Different?

  • SEAHIS is run “by owners for owners” as well as for companies which provide services to owners i.e. the entire hotel industry universe
  • It’s based around hotel real estate investors – Owners of hotel real estate have consistently been the largest group. In 2025, 52% of attendees came from groups which own and invest in hospitality real estate, both owners and owner-operators
  • It’s very high-level – usually 35-40% of attendees are company owners and their families, CEOs and other C-suite officers
  • Despite the name, this is an event which covers the entire Asia Pacific region, with sessions on, for example, Japan, Australia and the Maldives
  • Content-led – we are still very much a conference, not an exhibition. Generally half or more of our attendees are in the hall throughout the event and about 40% are speaking at some stage in proceedings
  • Value for money – SEAHIS is significantly lower cost than similar, older events without compromising on the content or the networking

* Source: SEAHIS 2026
