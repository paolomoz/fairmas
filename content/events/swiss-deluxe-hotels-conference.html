Fairmas will be represented by Alexander Engels, Director of Sales, as a featured speaker at the exclusive annual Swiss Deluxe Hotels conference for their Directors of Finance.

This exclusive event gathers top financial leaders from Switzerland’s most prestigious luxury hotels, offering a platform to discuss the latest trends, challenges, and innovations shaping the future of hospitality finance.

Alex will share valuable insights on the role of financial planning and intelligent data analysis in strengthening hotel performance and profitability. He will highlight how Fairmas supports hotels in navigating an increasingly dynamic market environment.

We look forward to connecting with the leaders of Swiss luxury hospitality and exploring new possibilities for financial excellence together.

When: September 22-23, 2025
Where: Victoria Jungfrau Grand Hotel, Interlaken, Switzerland
