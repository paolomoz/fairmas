Fairmas will be participating in the Tech in Hospitality Summit at the end of September.

In response to the growing demand for cutting-edge technology solutions in the hospitality sector, the Tech in Hospitality Summit, organized by Forum Events, offers a tailored experience for hospitality technology professionals and suppliers. This event is designed to connect innovative solution providers with industry professionals.

The summit features pre-arranged one-to-one meetings between hospitality professionals and suppliers, engaging speaker sessions, a networking dinner with entertainment, and full hospitality throughout.

Date: September 26, 2024
Location: Hilton London Canary Wharf

Alex Engels from Fairmas will showcase their hospitality financial planning & analysis software solutions, designed to help hotel businesses achieve their financial objectives and increase profitability. Attendees without pre-booked meetings or London-based hoteliers interested in learning more can also schedule a meeting directly with Alex.
