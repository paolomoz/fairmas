Fairmas will attend TTG Travel Experience, Italy’s leading B2B tourism marketplace, as a delegate. The event connects professionals from hospitality, travel, tourism and destination management to explore emerging trends, business opportunities and the future of global travel.

Date: October 14 – 16, 2026
Venue: Rimini Expo Centre, Rimini, Italy

Why meet Fairmas?

Connect with our team to discuss how better financial planning helps hotels respond to changing travel demand, optimize business performance and support sustainable growth in an increasingly competitive market.
If you’re attending TTG Travel Experience, we’d welcome the opportunity to exchange ideas and discuss how Fairmas helps hotels transform financial data into actionable business insights.

Book your meeting with Cornelia Kolm and Chiara Gianformaggio today and see how Fairmas can help elevate your financial strategy with their live demo.
