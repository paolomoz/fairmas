November 5, 2025 @ 9:00 am - November 6, 2025 @ 5:00 pm UTC-7

style
event-head

Fairmas will be participating in the Western Canadian Lodging Conference (WCLC), Western Canada’s premium hotel investment conference of the year. Focused on the hotel and resort sectors within Canada’s Western provinces, WCLC offers attendees an extensive economic and lodging overview, and insightful dialogue amongst industry leaders on the investment market, development and operational best practices, deal structuring, and which markets and asset types have the most potential. It is the ideal place to connect and explore the latest trends shaping the hospitality industry.

When: November 5-6, 2025
Where: Hyatt Regency, Vancouver, Canada

Why meet Fairmas:

  • See our solutions in action: Discover live demonstrations of our hotel-focused budgeting, forecasting, and reporting tools that help you stay ahead in a dynamic market.
  • Maximize your financial performance: Learn how Fairmas can support profitability and operational efficiency, tailored to the Canadian hospitality landscape.
  • Meet our experts: Connect directly with our team to discuss your business needs and explore customized solutions.

Meet Eric of the Fairmas North America team at our boothand start transforming your hotel’s financial strategy.
