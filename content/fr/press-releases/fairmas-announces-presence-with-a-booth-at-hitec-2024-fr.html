Fairmas announces presence with a booth at HITEC 2024

Kinza Raheel | 21/06/2024 | 3 min

Ce communiqué de presse est uniquement disponible en anglais.

Fairmas, a leading provider of financial planning, controlling, and management reporting software for the hospitality industry, is thrilled to announce its participation in HITEC 2024, the world’s largest and longest-running hospitality technology event. The event will take place from June 24-27 at the Charlotte Convention Center in Charlotte, North Carolina, USA. Fairmas will be showcasing its latest innovations at Booth 651.

Event Details:

  • Dates: June 24-27, 2024
  • Location: Charlotte Convention Center, Charlotte, N.C., USA
  • Fairmas Booth: 651

Among many others, one of the key highlights that Fairmas is happy to announce is the beta phase of the new HotStats 2-way interface. This cutting-edge feature allows hotels to seamlessly push Fairmas’ financial data to HotStats and pull the hotel’s most popular KPI’s and competitor set data from HotStats. This two-way communication capability significantly enhances data accuracy and efficiency, providing hoteliers with a more comprehensive understanding of their performance metrics.

In addition to the HotStats integration, Fairmas is excited to introduce its new Cash Flow Planning feature, which is currently in the beta testing phase as well. This innovative feature is designed to help hoteliers manage their cash flow more effectively, ensuring better financial stability and strategic planning.

Fairmas is also proud to announce its readiness to implement the latest edition of the Uniform System of Accounts for the Lodging Industry (USALI-12) *. Customers will soon be able to utilize the USALI-12* format in the Fairmas software, with the flexibility to switch to this new standard seamlessly.

“We are excited to bring new Fairmas features to HITEC 2024. Our team has been working diligently to develop solutions that meet the evolving needs of the hospitality industry. We look forward to demonstrating how our innovations can help hoteliers enhance their financial performance and efficiency.”

Niels Schroeder, Managing Director, Fairmas GmbH

HITEC 2024 attendees are invited to visit Fairmas at Booth 651 to learn more about these new features and explore how Fairmas can support their financial planning and reporting needs. Fairmas looks forward to connecting with old and new customers and demonstrating how their solutions can drive efficiency and profitability for your hotel.
