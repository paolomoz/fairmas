Fairmas Appoints Alexander Oehme as Vice President of Operations

Kinza Raheel | September 9, 2025 | 2 min

Fairmas, a leading provider of hospitality financial planning, controlling, and management reporting solutions worldwide, is pleased to announce the appointment of Alexander Oehme as Vice President, Operations. This strategic addition to the senior management team emphasizes Fairmas’ ongoing commitment to international expansion, operational excellence, and an enhanced customer experience.

Alexander brings more than 30 years of experience in the hospitality industry, having held multiple senior leadership roles across diverse markets in Europe. Most recently, he held the roles of Regional Manager, Motel One and Director Operations at Travel Charme Hotels & Resorts. His extensive hospitality-specific background in hotel operations and strategic leadership equips him with a deep understanding of the industry’s challenges, as well as the tools and innovation needed to drive performance and deliver value to customers.

In his new role at Fairmas, Alexander will lead the Customer Success Department, overseeing project management, customer support, and operations. He will play a vital role in strengthening internal processes while ensuring Fairmas continues to meet the evolving needs of its expanding global customer base and further deepening customer relationships.

‘We are pleased to welcome Alexander to Fairmas,’ said Niels Schroeder, Managing Director of Fairmas. “As we continue to grow and expand our global presence, it’s critical that we scale our operations with experienced leadership that understands both our industry and our customers. Alexander’s leadership experience, operational expertise and customer-centric mindset make him the perfect fit to help us deliver on our ambitious, yet realistic goals for the future.”

Commenting on his new role at Fairmas, Alexander shared: “I’m excited to join Fairmas at such a dynamic stage in its journey. Having worked in the industry for more than 30 years, I know the pain-points and pressures hoteliers face, first-hand. I look forward to strengthening the bridge between our customers and our team. This will ensure our solutions evolve with our customers’ needs and that our customer success efforts continue to deliver real, measurable value.”

Alexander’s appointment marks another important milestone in Fairmas’ growth. With a steadily expanding global footprint and a growing customer base, Fairmas is evolving to address rising industry and customer demands. The addition of experienced operational leadership like Alexander strengthens the company’s ability to deliver not just technology, but true partnership and support for its customers’ financial success.
