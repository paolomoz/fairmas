Fairmas celebrates 20th Anniversary

Kinza Raheel | June 20, 2023 | 2 min

Meet the Fairmas team at HITEC 2023 in Toronto

Fairmas, a leading provider of financial planning and analysis software for the hospitality industry, is delighted to commemorate its 20th anniversary and announce its presence at HITEC. Recognized as a trusted partner to hotels worldwide, Fairmas offers a comprehensive 360° Hospitality Financial BI Solution that empowers hoteliers to make informed financial decisions and optimize their operations.

Since its establishment in 2003, Fairmas has demonstrated a commitment to delivering cutting-edge technology solutions and exceptional service to its clients. Over the past two decades, the company has continuously evolved its suite of software products to meet the ever-changing needs of the hospitality industry. By providing insights, forecasting capabilities, and comprehensive reporting, Fairmas has become the go-to solution for hoteliers seeking to enhance financial performance and increase their profit.

As the most recent achievement, among many others, Fairmas has expanded its global presence with the successful rollout of its latest web-based software for IHG Hotels & Resorts in 75 countries, including 950 hotels globally. The achievement highlights the company’s dedication and ability to provide innovative solutions for the hospitality industry.

Furthermore, to meet the growing demand for its services in North America, Fairmas announced the opening of its new office in Montreal, Canada last year. This strategic move not only underscores the company’s dedication to serving its expanding client base in North America but also signifies Fairmas’ commitment to providing localized support and resources to its customers in the region. In relation to Fairmas’ expansion and increasing customer base in the region since the opening of the Fairmas North American entity, the Fairmas team will be present at HITEC in Toronto this year with their first ever company booth at a North American event.

“We are thrilled to celebrate our 20th anniversary and mark this significant milestone in our company’s history,” said Niels Schröder, Managing Director of Fairmas. “Our expansion into North America demonstrates our confidence in the growth opportunities presented by this market and our commitment to delivering exceptional service to our clients. We are excited to establish a local presence in North America by being present at HITEC and further strengthen our partnerships with hotels across the region.”

Niels Schröder, Managing Director, Fairmas GmbH

As part of its expansion plans, Fairmas’ participation at HITEC 2023 in Toronto, the world’s largest hospitality technology event, will be a steppingstone for more growth in the region. Held from June 26th to June 29th in Toronto, HITEC provides an ideal platform for industry professionals to connect, share knowledge, and explore the latest trends in hospitality technology. Fairmas will highlight its advanced financial planning and analysis software, emphasizing its ability to provide hotels with comprehensive insights and actionable intelligence to avoid errors, save time and increase their profit.

“As we celebrate Fairmas’ 20th anniversary, we are thrilled to also be present with a booth at HITEC Toronto, a milestone event for Fairmas. This prestigious platform provides us with an exceptional opportunity to showcase our unrivaled solutions to the North American hospitality market. With our innovative technology, extensive industry knowledge, and a legacy of excellence, we are confident in our ability to make a significant impact at HITEC Toronto and beyond.’’

John C. Dunn, President, Fairmas North America
