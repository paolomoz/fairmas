New White paper on partnership with Invesco Real Estate

Kinza Raheel | April 7, 2021 | 2 min

Fairmas publishes white paper on partnership with Invesco Real Estate for Hotel Asset Reporting

Fairmas, known for being thought-leaders in the hospitality industry and providing innovative BI Solutions for the hospitality industry, have created a digitalised asset management reporting solution. They have published a white paper in partnership with Invesco Real Estate on how the brainchild of Hotel Asset Reporting came into being and how Invesco Real Estate has benefited from the solution.

The white paper focuses on the necessity of digitalisation in the hospitality industry and connecting investors and operators to focus on what truly matters: Growing assets and increasing profits. The white paper answers the following question: How much time and money can industries invest in manual data processes?

Download the white paper now

Asset Managers have found themselves investing more time trying to handle the manual processes of data management than focusing on investigating key insights. Digitalised asset management reporting has enabled asset managers to bridge the gap between investors and operators and has played a major role during the pandemic. The promising partnership between Fairmas and Invesco Real Estate is a step to a better future for the hospitality industry and Fairmas would like to thank Invesco Real Estate for their support.

Inside the white paper

Fairmas had been a trusted hospitality BI solution provider for Invesco Real Estate, a global real estate investment manager, since 2014. Due to a lack of standardization in reporting from hotel operators, a variety of Chart of Accounts and different file formats in Excel, asset managers faced an overall data jungle. Upon mutually discovering the need for a solution in 2019, Fairmas set out to create a service that takes over the manual processes of data management while the asset manager focuses on the analysis of the data. The Hotel Asset Reporting solution created by Fairmas provides customized KPI reporting that is available 24/7, and the quarterly analytics prepared by Fairmas have been a key element supporting Invesco and asset managers in creating fund reporting.

“Digitalization is the future of the hospitality industry and its financial processes. Our successful partnership with Fairmas has reduced our Asset Managers’ workload by 80% and we can wholeheartedly recommend it to everyone,” says Hans-Peter Hermann, Senior Director, Hotel Investment Management, Europe, Invesco Real Estate. A win-win solution for operators, investors and asset managers, Hotel Asset Reporting reduces workload and allows everyone to focus on what really matters – a future of growth and profit.
