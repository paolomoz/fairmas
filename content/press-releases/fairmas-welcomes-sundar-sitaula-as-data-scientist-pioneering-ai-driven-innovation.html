Fairmas welcomes Sundar Sitaula as Data Scientist, pioneering AI-driven innovation

March 27, 2025 | July 2, 2026 | 5 min read

Berlin, 03/27/2025

Fairmas, a leading provider of financial planning and reporting solutions for the hospitality industry, proudly announces the appointment of Sundar Sitaula as its new Data Scientist.

With over five years of expertise in AI and machine learning, Sundar’s addition further highlights Fairmas’ commitment to advancing innovation in financial technology and AI for the hospitality sector. Sundar brings a wealth of experience in predictive analytics and data-driven solutions, having successfully led data science initiatives across multiple industries. His proven ability to build and lead data-focused teams aligns perfectly with Fairmas’ vision for expanding its AI capabilities.

In industries as inherently data driven as finance and hospitality, AI has a huge transformative potential. As hotels generate vast amounts of data daily, AI can analyze this data to uncover hidden patterns, enabling more accurate decision-making.

“The hospitality industry faces unique challenges that require innovative solutions,” Sundar said. “By leveraging AI, we can automate complex and tedious processes, improve forecasting accuracy, and provide actionable recommendations. My focus at Fairmas will be on structuring unstructured data and automating certain processes, tasks that are often time-consuming but essential for data-driven decision-making,” he further added.

Traditional budget planning, forecasting, and decision-making require extensive manual effort and financial expertise. Analyzing large volumes of historical data is not only time-consuming but also prone to errors. AI, combined with financial and industry knowledge, can seamlessly analyze real-time data, identify patterns, and predict future trends with remarkable accuracy.

According to Niels Schroeder, Managing Director of Fairmas, “The addition of Sundar to the Fairmas team marks a significant leap toward realizing our vision for AI. With Sundar’s expertise, we aim to empower our customers, redefining financial planning and decision-making for the hospitality industry.”

He further emphasized, “We believe that by combining human expertise with data-driven intelligence, we can offer unparalleled value to our users with AI.”

Fairmas warmly welcomes Sundar Sitaula and looks forward to a future where AI revolutionizes financial planning and decision-making in hospitality. As we continue to push the boundaries of what technology can achieve in hospitality finance, Sundar’s expertise will be instrumental in shaping smarter, more efficient solutions. Together, we look forward to transforming data into actionable intelligence, helping hotels make more informed, strategic financial decisions with confidence.
