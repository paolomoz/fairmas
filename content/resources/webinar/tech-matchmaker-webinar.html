Maximize Hospitality Success with Fairmas’ Business Intelligence Software Solution

Meet Oliver Rabe, Chief Commercial Officer at Fairmas

Join Oliver Rabe, a hospitality industry expert and Chief Commercial Officer of Fairmas, as he shares expertise in hotel financial planning. Since joining Fairmas in 2014, Oliver has driven product innovation, leading the development and launch of Fairmas’ hotel financial planning tool, now a key resource for hoteliers worldwide. With over 15 years in international hospitality, Oliver understands the critical needs of the industry and helps shape Fairmas’ strategic approach to meet them.

Why Choose Fairmas for Hotel Financial Planning?

Since 2003, Fairmas has been a trusted provider of BI hospitality software solutions, specializing in financial planning, controlling, and management for the hotel industry. Today, over 5,500 hotels and 18,000+ users across 115 countries rely on Fairmas for tools that enhance hotel profitability and financial insights. Fairmas’ software helps hotel professionals analyze data, consolidate it with ease, enhance the hotel financial planning and analysis process and make informed decisions to stay competitive.
