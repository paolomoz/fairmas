FairPlanner

FairPlanner

FairPlanner is a comprehensive hotel Financial Planning & Analysis (FP&A) software designed to simplify budgeting, forecasting, and profitability planning across your entire hotel. Plan revenue, expenses, payroll, and profits within one centralized software while creating multiple planning scenarios and comparing outcomes with ease. Integrate financial planning data with actual accounting figures and PMS booking information to maintain an accurate, real-time financial outlook. From departmental planning to portfolio-wide consolidation, FairPlanner provides hotel finance teams with a complete financial view that supports faster decisions and long-term profitability.

You can also plan all relevant costs & salary per employee and per department that result in precise analyses for all management levels. Furthermore, perform operational consolidation & portfolio performance analysis for all your hotels.

Cash Flow Planning

Cash Flow Planning

The Cash Flow Planning module extends FairPlanner with powerful tools for managing hotel operating cash flow and working capital. Plan cash inflows and outflows using integrated balance sheet accounts while maintaining full visibility of your financial position. Flexible planning rules, customizable balance sheet structures, Excel exports, currency conversion, and property-specific planning make cash flow management simple and reliable. Hotels gain greater confidence in every financial decision.

Cash Flow Planning

Revenue Planning & Analytics

Revenue Planning & Analytics

Revenue Planning & Analytics (RP&A) enables hotels to connect revenue management with financial planning through one intelligent software. Integrations with PMS, RMS, rate shopping solutions, and benchmarking tools support accurate daily and monthly revenue forecasts across every profit centre. Analyse on-the-books reservations, pickup trends, and market segmentation to identify opportunities earlier and improve forecasting accuracy. Flexible planning scenarios, advanced reporting, mobile access, and the Revenue Planning Assistant help revenue and finance teams work together more efficiently and make data-driven decisions.

Hotel Asset Reporting

Hotel Asset Reporting

Fairmas simplifies hotel asset management and reporting by replacing manual data collection with a standardized reporting processes. Asset managers gain fast access to reliable financial information, allowing them to spend less time preparing reports and more time analysing portfolio performance.

By reducing manual reporting effort by up to 80%, Hotel Asset Reporting improves efficiency, strengthens data consistency, and provides the insights needed to support strategic investment and operational decisions across hotel portfolios.
