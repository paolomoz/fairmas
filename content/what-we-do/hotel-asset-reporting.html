Why Hotel Asset Reporting?

As a hotel investor or asset manager, you know the struggle: endless spreadsheets, inconsistent reports from operators, and countless hours spent reconciling numbers. Hotel Asset Reporting by Fairmas eliminates that chaos. Our web-based platform automates the collection, standardization, and consolidation of hotel performance data, giving you clean, comparable insights you can trust. The result: less manual work, faster reporting, and more time to focus on driving asset value.

Free Up to 80% of Your Time

Say goodbye to endless data gathering and manual processing. Fairmas takes care of communication, data collection, and consolidation. This reduces your workload by up to 80% and gives you more time to analyze and act.

One Version of the Truth

No more raw, inconsistent data from different operators, charts of accounts, or systems. Hotel Asset Reporting delivers standardized, consolidated performance reports across your entire portfolio, with automated currency conversion and multiple time horizons (daily to yearly).

Smarter Scenario Comparisons

Quickly compare actuals, budgets, forecasts, on-the-books, and long-range scenarios. Instantly evaluate risks, opportunities, and success probabilities before making key decisions.

KPIs That Actually Matter

Access at-a-glance KPIs designed for comparability across brands and operators. Gain reliable benchmarks and actionable insights that help you boost profitability and performance.

Anytime, Anywhere Access - Backed by Experts

Work securely from anywhere with our always-updated, web-based solution. And because we’re hotel reporting experts, our support team is with you every step of the way, from implementation to ongoing success.
