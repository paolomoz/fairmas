Careers at Fairmas

Join an international team shaping the future of hospitality technology

Grow with us.

Join Fairmas!

At Fairmas, our people are the driving force behind our success. We believe in honesty, integrity, and teamwork, and we create an environment where your ideas matter and your work has real impact.

We are an international software company helping hotels worldwide make smarter financial decisions. If you are looking for a workplace where you can learn, grow, and contribute, Fairmas offers the support and opportunities to build a meaningful career.

Are you ready to make a difference in a fast-growing, international technology company?

Do you want to work in a dynamic environment that encourages learning, innovation, and professional growth?

If you are passionate about collaboration and want to contribute to shaping the future of hospitality financial performance management, Fairmas could be the right place for you.

Explore our open positions or contact our HR team to learn more about career opportunities at Fairmas.

Berlin Headquarters

Full-Stack Software Developer (all genders)

Become part of Fairmas as a Full-Stack Software Developer and help shape the future of our SaaS platform. From intuitive frontends to robust backend services, you'll build features end-to-end, solve real-world challenges, and contribute to software used by international hotel teams. Based in Berlin/Brandenburg, full-time or part-time, Monday to Friday, with work-life balance guaranteed.

Apply now

Software Implementation Specialist (all genders) – Hotel Software & Controlling

Join Fairmas as a Software Implementation Specialist for software projects (SaaS) in the hospitality industry. Do you come from the hospitality industry, the finance department of a hotel, or hospitality management, and want to use your experience in an innovative software company specializing in hotel software and financial planning? Then Fairmas is the right place for you. Location: wherever you are – nationwide in Germany, Monday to Friday.

Apply now

What we have in store for you

  • Balance: No weekend work, no shifts, no overtime.
  • Appreciation: Fair pay and a safe working environment.
  • Flexibility: Work remotely and from our modern Berlin office.
  • Understanding: Many of us come from the hotel industry ourselves.
  • Purpose: Help hotels around the world stay economically successful.
  • Sustainability: Our offices are eco-friendly and perfectly connected.
  • Mobility: We subsidize your Deutschlandticket.
  • Growth: Through team events and personal development – we support what makes you unique.

You don’t meet every single requirement or have gaps in your resume? No problem! Apply anyway – what matters most to us are your motivation, values, and personality.
