Partners

Creating a future together

Fairmas has a wide range of strategic partners and a strong network, working closely with associations and organizations across the hospitality industry.

Innovation, customer satisfaction and customer benefits are the backbone of Fairmas. We are constantly evolving and developing our software solutions to abridge the gaps in the hospitality industry. We take pride in our strategic partners and our strong network. Their open involvement and experience help us optimize our products and expand our portfolio appropriately in a fast-paced environment.

Our network of strategic partners and cooperations includes berner+becker, the British Columbia Hotel Association, the Hotel Association of Canada, HAS-360, HRS – Hospitality & Retail Systems, the Irish Hotels Federation, IT.DS Beratung, LucaNet, Regiomarktplatz Consulting, Swiss Hospitality Solutions, the SHS Academy, HFTP (Hospitality Financial and Technology Professionals) and JF Hospitality.

Part of the Fairmas family

Whether you are a customer, partner or employee, we make sure you feel like a part of the Fairmas family. We embody the core principles of the hospitality industry – commitment, communication and competence – and make sure to enable your growth. Our vision is to be the trusted partner empowering the world of hospitality to lead the future.

As hospitality experts, we understand the ever-evolving needs of each customer, enhancing their ability to maximise profitability. We provide innovative and easy to integrate software solutions for financial planning and analysis, combined with our personalised service.

Alongside these strategic partnerships, Fairmas also connects with your existing IT setup, enabling data transfers across 200+ systems, including PMS, ERP, RMS, DMS, benchmarking tools and rate shoppers. You can find the full list of technology partners under Integrations, and learn more about the company on the About Fairmas page.
